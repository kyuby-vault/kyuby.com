# Hello, kyuby!

Welcome to **kyuby.com** – our home on the edge of the internet.

## What is kyuby?

kyuby is a platform built around edge-first infrastructure principles. We believe that latency is a feature, not a bug – and that pushing compute closer to users is the right way to build modern web services.

## What to expect

Over the coming weeks, we'll be sharing:

- Technical deep-dives on edge computing and CDN architecture
- Open-source tools and libraries we've built
- Infrastructure patterns that scale gracefully

Stay tuned, and welcome aboard! 🐱
